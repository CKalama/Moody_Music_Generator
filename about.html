🎵 Welcome! 🎵

. . . and thanks for visiting our website! We have created this platform to provide our users with a simple service: song recommendations based how they're feeling! First, consider the mood you are in. Next, scan our various buttons and choose the one that best fits said mood.

Our state-of-the-art algorithm will return a song recommendation that you will (hopefully) love! If you'd like another song, click the button again. If your mood changes, click a different button. Enjoy 😁
